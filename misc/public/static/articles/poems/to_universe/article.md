# To Universe

Written by Alexander Tokar on July 12th, 2025

```txt
Universe!
O Holy!

Whisper something!
Whilst I shout!

O Universe!
You puzzling thing!

Forever young!
Like a kid whose parents grief!

Silent!
Like snow covered in ashes!

Careless!
Like justness soaping its hands!

Whisper not.
Whilst crickets chirp.
```
